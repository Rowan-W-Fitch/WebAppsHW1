Color Blindness UI/UX Approaches

  • Medium.com
  • Add Patterns and Textures to emphasize contrast between objects
  • Implement Color with Caption or Icon
  • Usabilla.com
  • Avoid Color Combinations that are hard for color blind people to discern (ie green and red or blue and grey)
  • Use as few colors as possible
  • Secretstache.com
  • Underline Links
  • Mark required form fields with asterisk
